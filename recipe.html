Mangu con Salami y Queso Frito

Description

Mangu is a traditional Dominican breakfast food consisting of mashed plantains and usually topped with pan fried onions. It is typically served with salami and fried tropical cheese. Find a similar recipe here

Ingredients

  • Plantains
  • Onions
  • Salami
  • Fried cheese

Directions

Steps to Prepare Plantains

  1. Put a pot of water to boil
  2. Add salt and 3 plantains to boiling water
  3. Let plantains boil for 15 minutes or until tender

Steps to Prepare Cheese

  1. Cut tropical cheese into about quarter-inch slices
  2. Add vegetable oil to a pan over medium heat
  3. Fry cheese until it reaches a golden brown or desired color
  4. Enjoy!
